The Shinkansen, colloquially known in English as the bullet train, is a network of high-speed railway lines in Japan

Types of train

This is the 700 series of passenger train which was introduced in 1999

This is the 800 series of passenger train which is in service since 2004 on Tsubame services.
This is the W7 series of passenger train which have 12-car trains operated on the Hokuriku Shinkansen since March 2015.
This is the E6 series of passenger train which was introduced in March 2013 on Akita Shinkansen Komachi services.
